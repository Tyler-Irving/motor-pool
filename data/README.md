# data/

## eval/heldout_v1.jsonl (committed)

The frozen evaluation set. Hand-built and hand-verified. Committed to version
control because it is text-only and must not change between runs. Each line is
one item with a gold label (answerable or refuse) and, for answerable items, the
gold supporting section(s) used to score citation correctness.

The eval items are drawn from TM sections that are excluded from the
data-generation pool, so no procedure seen during training appears in eval. This
section-level disjointness is asserted by a test and is what keeps the
base-versus-finetuned comparison honest.

Strata: answerable-procedural, answerable-factual, hard-negative refusal,
out-of-scope refusal, ambiguous refusal.

## train/ (gitignored, regenerable)

Generated by `motor-pool gen-data`. Holds train.jsonl and val.jsonl plus a
data-generation report. Not committed: it is large, regenerable, and a product
of a frontier teacher. The train/val split is 90/10 of the non-eval sections,
stratified by bucket and section-disjoint from eval.
